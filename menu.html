Players are not allowed to discard a card with a lower value than the one on top of the discard pile

If a player does not have a card in hand that is higher in value than the last card played on the table, they must take the entire discard pile into their hand.

If the last card on the discard pile is a 9 of hearts, the player only draws 3 cards from the pile.

The player who successfully discards all their cards first wins, when the dealer's deck is empty.
